What is LayBack's Return Policy?

We give you 30 days to try out your LayBack purchase to make sure it works for you.

Here's what you need to know:

  • You can return within 30 days of an online order delivery or LayBack store purchase (some exceptions apply).
  • Proof of purchase is required to return.
  • Items purchased at LayBack Clearance stores cannot be returned, and LayBackClearance stores do not accept returns of any kind.
  • Returns are free for LayBack Members.

So go ahead, shop with confidence and enjoy your 30-day trial.

How to Return Your Items

1

Prepare Your Return

Package your items with all original tags and packaging.

2

Start Your Return

Fill out the return form online or include your receipt.

3

Ship or Drop Off

Return by mail or at any LayBack store location.

Condition of Returns

All items must be returned unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached. Items that show signs of wear or have been altered may not be accepted for return or exchange.

Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive your return. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please note that shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to a LAYBACK error or defective merchandise.

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size or color, you can request this during the return process. Alternatively, for faster service, we recommend returning the original item and placing a new order.

International Returns

For international orders, please contact our customer service team for specific return instructions as processes may vary by country.
